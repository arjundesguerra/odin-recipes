Steak

Description

Steak is a tender, juicy, and flavorful piece of cooked beef. There are many different cuts of steak and this recipe will work for all of those cuts.

Ingredients

  • Any cut of steak
  • Salt and pepper
  • High smoke point oil (canola, avocado, peanut)

Steps

  1. Make sure steak is fully thawed
  2. Season generously with salt and pepper (especially if the steak is thick)
  3. Heat up pan with oil on the highest setting
  4. Lay steak down and flip every two minutes
  5. Once steak is at 125F internal temperature, it is done
  6. Let it rest for five minutes and enjoy!
