Chicken Parm

Description

Chicken Parm is a chicken cutlet (can be breast or thigh) covered in a crust, fried, and topped with tomato sauce and cheese

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast or thigh
  • San Marzano tomatoes
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Panko breadcrumbs

Steps:

  1. Coat chicken in breadcrumbs
  2. Fry on medium heat until golden brown
  3. Season tomato sauce with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Lay fried chicken on sheetpan and pour sauce over the chicken
  5. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top
  6. Broil chicken in the oven until cheese is melted
